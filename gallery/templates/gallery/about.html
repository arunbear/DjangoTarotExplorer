About

The Waite-Smith Tarot includes a great deal of visual detail in each of the cards. This website collects the images together into various galleries so that the images can be compared and contrasted with one another.

This should be of particular interest to students of the Tarot seeking to develop intuition based primarily on the contents of the images themselves, rather than on traditional meanings.
